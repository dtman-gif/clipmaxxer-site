clipmaxxer

Personal tool that turns livestream VODs into short-form clips and posts them to the creator's own TikTok Drafts inbox for manual publishing.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

clipmaxxer is a self-hosted personal tool. When you connect your TikTok account, clipmaxxer stores the OAuth access token and open_id locally on the device running the tool so it can upload clips to your own Drafts inbox on your behalf.

What we collect

  • Your TikTok open_id and access_token (stored only on your own machine; never transmitted elsewhere).
  • Metadata about clips you generate: captions, timestamps, virality scores — stored locally in a SQLite database.

What we don't collect

  • We never send your TikTok credentials to any third party.
  • We never collect analytics about you or your viewers.
  • We do not sell or share any data.

Deleting your data

Revoke the app's access at tiktok.com/setting/apps and delete the local .tiktok_tokens.json file to remove all stored credentials.
